WHY A NOTEPAD, NOT A SPREADSHEET: speed. When you're filming or commenting, you need to grab a link instantly. A flat text file opens anywhere, syncs everywhere, and never makes you hunt across columns. One file = your whole money map.

▸ STEP 4

Monetize with short-form content

Links don't earn until people see them. The engine is short-form video — TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts — and the cheat code is simple: don't invent, copy what already works. Find the formats that are already going viral in this niche, remake them with your spin and your link, and post every day.

THE ONE LAW HERE: the first 3 seconds decide everything. You're not being original — you're being consistent with a proven format. Model the structure, swap in your hook, your offer, your link. Volume + iteration wins.
▶ set up your social accounts (then log them in my-socials.txt)
  1. Pick a niche per account: "free money apps," "side hustles," "passive income," "casino wins." One angle, stick to it — the algorithm rewards focus.
  2. Make the accounts: TikTok, Instagram (Reels), YouTube (Shorts). Same handle everywhere. Clean bio that says what you post + a link.
  3. Link hub in bio: set up a free Linktree/Beacons, drop ALL your referral links in it, and put that one URL in every bio + pinned comment.
  4. Log it: fill templates/my-socials.txt — handle, niche, link-in-bio, and the 3 accounts you'll model.

▸ THE COPY-WHAT-WORKS METHOD

  1. Find the winners. Search your niche on TikTok/Reels. Look for videos with way more views than the creator's normal — that's a format that's working, not just a big account.
  2. Screenshot the structure. Note the hook (first line/visual), the pacing, the on-screen text, the call-to-action. That skeleton is what you're copying — never the actual clip.
  3. Remake it with your spin. Same structure, your face/voice/screen-recording, your offer ("I use this app to earn X — link in bio"). Show the app, the bonus, the payout.
  4. CTA every time. "Link in my bio" + pin a comment with the link. Make the next step obvious.
  5. Post daily, study the data. Keep the formats that pop, kill the ones that don't. 1 in 10 hitting is a win — that one funds everything.
▶ let the machine do the heavy lifting

You already own Opus Pro / OpusClip (Step 2). Use it to slice long videos into dozens of shorts, auto-caption, and reframe vertical — then post across all platforms. The CASH.BOT CONTENT modules (VIRALSHORTS, CAPTIONS, VIDEOHOOKS, THUMBNAILIDEA) generate hooks, scripts, and captions in bulk so you're never staring at a blank screen. One list in → a month of posts out.

▸ THE WEEKLY LOOP

  • Mon: find 5 winning formats in your niche, save them.
  • Tue–Thu: remake + post 1–3 per day across TikTok / Reels / Shorts.
  • Fri: check which links got signups (your notepad + dashboards), double down on what paid.
  • Repeat. Every new follower is another set of eyes on every link in your bio.
STAY HONEST + COMPLIANT. Disclose affiliate/referral links (it's required and it builds trust). Don't promise guaranteed money — show what's real. Follow each platform's rules on gambling/ financial content (some restrict casino promos or require age-gating). Honest creators last; spammers get banned and lose every link at once.
