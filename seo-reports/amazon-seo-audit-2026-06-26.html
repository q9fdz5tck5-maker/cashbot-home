  2. amazon.com (homepage)
    Add H1 tag: "Online Shopping for Electronics, Books, Clothing & More" (visually styled small or screen-reader-accessible)
    Homepage has no H1 — a basic missing signal for the 1M/mo "online shopping" keyword where Amazon ranks ~#6. Google uses H1 as a primary on-page relevance indicator. This is the single lowest-effort change for the highest-volume keyword in the audit.
    ⏱ ~10 min
  3. amazon.com/b?ie=UTF8&node=2735182011 (Kindle eBooks Advanced Search)
    Add rel=canonical pointing to amazon.com/kindle-dbs/storefront
    One of 4 Amazon pages competing for "kindle ebooks," splitting ranking authority. This canonical tag takes 5 minutes to add and begins consolidating link equity into the primary storefront. Amazon already ranks #1–2 — consolidation locks in that position.

  4. amazon.com/books
    Update title tag: To "Buy Books Online | New, Used & Rare Books | Amazon" (51 chars)

  5. amazon.com/amazonprime
    Add FAQPage JSON-LD schema with 5 questions (cost, free trial, 2-day shipping, Prime Video, cancellation)

  7. amazon.com/gp/help/.../GFUT24ALVAVC6VFD (Same-Day delivery help article)
    Add rel=canonical → /fmc/ssd-storefront + add "Shop Same-Day Items" CTA button
    Help article competes with the commercial page for the same keyword, diluting PageRank. Canonical tag resolves authority split; CTA button converts an informational dead-end into a purchase funnel touchpoint. Two changes, 10 minutes total.
