===========================================
You don't write the videos. The AI does.
Open any AI (ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini — free versions all work),
paste a prompt below, and swap the [BRACKETS] for your offer.

HOW TO USE:
1) Pick ONE app with a free offer (free coins, free cash, free gift card).
2) Paste a prompt. Fill the brackets.

4) Film it in 30 seconds (or screen-record the app). Post it.

PROMPT 1 — 30 VIDEO IDEAS (start here)
===========================================
You are a short-form content strategist.
I promote [APP NAME], which gives new users [FREE OFFER].
My referral link earns me [WHAT YOU EARN] when someone signs up.
Give me 30 short-form video ideas (TikTok / Reels / Shorts) that would make
someone want to sign up using my link.
Make them simple to film on a phone in under 60 seconds.
For each idea give: the angle, and a one-line hook.
Keep the language plain. No hype words.

===========================================
PROMPT 2 — SCROLL-STOPPING HOOKS
===========================================
Write me 10 opening lines (the first 3 seconds) for a short video about
[APP NAME] that gives [FREE OFFER].
The goal: make someone stop scrolling instantly.
Make them sound like a real person, not an ad.
Plain words. Short. Punchy.

===========================================
PROMPT 3 — FULL 30-SECOND SCRIPT
===========================================
Write a 30-second short-form video script about [APP NAME].
It gives new users [FREE OFFER]. I earn [WHAT YOU EARN] per signup.
Structure:
- HOOK (first 3 seconds — stop the scroll)
- THE MIDDLE (show the free offer, keep it honest and simple)
- CALL TO ACTION ("link in my bio", "comment X")
Tell me exactly what to say and what to show on screen.
No fake promises. Plain English. I should be able to film this in one take.

===========================================
PROMPT 4 — CAPTION + HASHTAGS
===========================================
Write a short caption for a video about [APP NAME] ([FREE OFFER]).
Include a clear call to action to use my link in bio.
Add a line that honestly says it's a referral link.
Then give me 12 relevant hashtags for [TikTok / Reels / Shorts].

===========================================
PROMPT 5 — COPY WHAT WORKS (paste a winning video)
===========================================
Here's a short video that's going viral in my niche: [PASTE THE IDEA / TRANSCRIPT].
Rewrite it so I can use the same format to promote [APP NAME] ([FREE OFFER]).
Keep the structure that made it work. Change the topic to my offer.
Give me the new hook, script, and caption. Plain words.
