MY SOCIAL MEDIA ACCOUNTS — POSTING NOTEPAD
==========================================
One block per platform. This is where your referral links get SEEN.
The play: short-form content (Reels / TikTok / Shorts) → link in bio /
pinned comment → people sign up under YOUR referral link.

RULES OF THE GAME (read once, live by them):
- One niche per account. Pick the angle: "free money / app hacks",
  "side hustle", "passive income", "casino wins", etc. Stay on it.
- Model what already works. Find 5 videos in your niche with way more
  views than the creator's average. Re-make that format with YOUR spin
  and YOUR link. Copy the STRUCTURE, never steal the clip.
- Link in bio + pinned comment on every video. Use a Linktree/Beacons
  so all your referral links live in one tap.
- Post daily. Volume + iteration beats one "perfect" video.
- Disclose: say it's a referral/affiliate link. Honest = sustainable.
